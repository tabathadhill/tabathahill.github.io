Thinking before building

01 — DIAGNOSE
Find the real gap

I start with the actual performance problem — not the symptom. What are people doing vs. what they should be doing? That gap is where design begins.

02 — DESIGN
Map to outcomes

Learning objectives mapped to business results. Right modality, right medium — eLearning, video, job aids, or AI-personalized paths.

03 — BUILD
Craft the experience

High-fidelity content with real scenario practice, visual storytelling, and interactions people actually want to engage with.

04 — MEASURE
Prove the impact

Completion rates are a floor. I track behavior change, connect learning to KPIs, and report in language executives understand.
