Case Study 05

Cybersecurity Compliance
Training Program Development

Cybersecurity Awareness NIST · FISMA · FedRAMP SCORM Development ISO · SOC · HITRUST Section 508 Accessibility SME Collaboration

As the sole L&D professional for a global cybersecurity compliance organization, I designed, built, and continue to maintain a suite of compliance training programs — spanning Federal practice onboarding, annual security awareness, and framework-specific training for ISO, SOC, and HITRUST departments — ensuring 100+ employees are audit-ready, policy-compliant, and continuously upskilled.

Context & Business Need

A fast-growing cybersecurity compliance organization needed consistent, framework-aligned training for employees working across Federal, ISO, SOC, and HITRUST practices. New hires required structured onboarding that covered both organizational processes and the specific compliance frameworks governing their work.

Existing employees needed annual refreshers that kept pace with evolving policy, regulatory updates, and emerging cybersecurity requirements — all tracked and reportable to Associate Director and Director level leadership.

Onboard 100+ Federal practice new hires to FISMA, FedRAMP, and NIST frameworks quickly and consistently
Develop required annual compliance training for ISO, SOC, and HITRUST departments
Maintain all programs yearly — incorporating policy changes, SME input, and AI-assisted content updates
Ensure Section 508 accessibility compliance across all published training materials
Track completion rates and deliver regular reporting to Associate Director and Director level leadership
Integrate a shadow project component into Federal new hire onboarding to bridge knowledge to real-world application

My Role

I served as Sole Compliance Training Architect and Program Manager — owning the full lifecycle of each program from needs analysis and content development through annual review, accessibility compliance, LMS administration, and leadership reporting.

I collaborated with SMEs across Federal, ISO, SOC, and HITRUST practices, and leveraged AI tools to accelerate content updates and maintain program currency without sacrificing instructional quality.

Approach

01

Federal University — New Hire Onboarding Program

Designed and built Federal University from scratch — a multi-hour, multi-day onboarding program for Federal practice new hires covering FISMA, FedRAMP, NIST frameworks, organizational processes, and role expectations. Integrated a shadow project component to bridge knowledge to real-world application. Developed SCORM-packaged eLearning and supporting materials delivered across two days to support knowledge retention and learner readiness.

02

Framework-Specific Compliance Training — ISO, SOC & HITRUST

Developed required compliance training for ISO, SOC, and HITRUST departments — building both new hire required courses and annual refresher programs for existing employees. Each program was tailored to the specific framework requirements and role responsibilities of each practice area, ensuring content was accurate, applicable, and audit-ready.

03

Annual Review Cycle with SME Collaboration & AI Integration

Established a yearly content review process — partnering with SMEs across each compliance practice to validate accuracy, incorporate regulatory updates, and apply policy changes. Integrated AI tools to accelerate content revision workflows, identify gaps, and maintain program currency without sacrificing instructional quality.

04

Section 508 Accessibility Compliance

Applied Section 508 accessibility standards to all training materials prior to publishing — reviewing content for compliance, resolving identified issues, and ensuring all learners across the organization could access and complete required training regardless of ability.

05

LMS Administration, Completion Tracking & Leadership Reporting

Administered all programs within the LMS — managing assignments, tracking completion rates, enforcing required training deadlines, and generating reports for Associate Director and Director level stakeholders. Maintained consistently high completion rates by building accountability touchpoints into the program structure.

Outcomes

100+ Federal practice employees onboarded through a consistent, framework-aligned program
High completion rates maintained across ISO, SOC, HITRUST, and Federal departments
Audit-ready workforce equipped with current, accurate compliance knowledge
All published training materials meet Section 508 federal accessibility standards
Regular completion reports delivered to Associate Director and Director level leadership
Scalable program infrastructure built to grow with the organization and adapt to annual policy changes
